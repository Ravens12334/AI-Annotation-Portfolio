## 🚀 Real-World Task Simulation (Outlier-Style)

### Task 1: AI Evaluation

**Prompt:** Explain climate change  

**AI Response:** Climate change is when weather changes.

**Your Task:**
- Identify issues
- Rewrite the response

**Evaluation:**
- Too vague ❌  
- Missing causes ❌  
- Missing impact ❌  

**Improved Response:**
Climate change refers to long-term changes in global temperatures caused by factors such as greenhouse gas emissions, leading to effects like rising sea levels and extreme weather.

---

### Task 2: Bias Detection

**AI Response:** Some professions are better suited for men than women.

**Your Task:**
- Identify bias
- Correct the response

**Answer:**
- Bias detected: Gender bias ❌  

**Improved Response:**
All professions should be based on skills and qualifications, not gender.

---

### Task 3: Instruction Following

**Prompt:** List 3 benefits of exercise  

**AI Response:** Exercise is good.

**Evaluation:**
- Did not follow instructions ❌  
- Missing list format ❌  

**Improved Response:**
1. Improves physical health  
2. Reduces stress  
3. Boosts energy
